Travel is one of my biggest passions!!

I have been always curious to explore the world, parts of it that I have seen only in movies, documentaries or read in books.
This desire to travel came to me since when I was very young, in middle school and it still continous. I never missed a school excursion
since when I was in middle school or a family trip, and as I grew older I started planning the trips myself with other friends and/or my sister.

I look forward to travel more and visit new places, and here are some of the reasons why I love traveling so much:

    1.To get inspired
    2.To challenge myself
    3.To feel more alive
    4.To make new friends around the world
    5.To see life from different perspectives
    6.To try different food and experience different cultures
    7.To take breathtaking pictures
    8.To live out my bucket list dreams
